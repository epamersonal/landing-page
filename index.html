Building on Experience

The team behind our MAINFINEX exchange consists of successful traders. Our success gives us an understanding of the tools and resources that regular traders need to convert their passion for trading into a profession.

The Most Trusted Digital Assts Exchange

With so many exchanges for trading cryptocurrency all offering similar services, choosing which platform to use is complicated. MAINFINEX is positioned to offer a clear, simple exchange that traders and investors can easily use. We hope that this simplicity and ease of use will help us become the main crypto exchange, hence the name MAINFINEX. From there, we reward traders for choosing our platform with a rich functionality that follows the best current practices and a user-friendly interface.

All of the exchange features and functionality are designed based on the needs of traders, guided by our team’s experience

Incorporating Gaming Elements for Familiarity

The emergence of cryptocurrency has transformed the world of finance. This transformation can lead to confusion for newcomers as well as those with financial experience. We understand that users of our exchange need clear, relevant features and tools. We took a closer look at our main audience, which is made up of people between 25 and 38 years old. This group likely played “DOOM 2” as students in 1994. We took this common gaming experience from our youth as inspiration to make the exchange more user-friendly and understandable, regardless of someone’s crypto-trading experience. We decided to give simple names to complex financial instruments. This helps crypto traders separate themselves from the investment bankers in suits who played dirty tricks and led to the mortgage crisis. Our exchange offers the most popular financial instruments available. From there, we make them easy to understand and activate in just a click. This way, trading on our exchange is just as simple as the gaming of your childhood. We keep things simple and familiar for a shallow learning curve, letting our users begin trading and making a profit quickly.

Unsuccessful Traders Lose Money on Other Exchanges

Every statistic seems to show a different figure, but there is a significant trend of unsuccessful traders losing money. The consensus seems that at least 70 percent of traders end up losing funds on exchange transactions. We want to help our clients avoid becoming part of this statistic.

Thanks to our experience as successful traders before creating the exchange, we understand the most useful trading mechanisms. This lets us provide our clients with those trading mechanisms and other related tools. The result is helping our traders make a profit, instead of becoming part of the statistics regarding unsuccessful traders.

Token Burning

Our exchange will follow the common mechanism of token burning. Each quarter, we will spend ___% of our consolidated gross income on buying and burning tokens. We will continue this process until 90 percent of our tokens (8.1 million tokens) are burned. The remaining 900,000 tokens will stay in circulation.

“Work for Us”

Our team understands that not everyone with a passion for cryptocurrency trading has the funds available to spend their time trading. As such, we offer opportunities for traders who have free working hours but lack substantial deposits. These traders can perform important tasks for our exchange and receive rewards. This idea is similar to that of Amazon Mechanical Turk and lets all traders be involved in the exchange, regardless of their deposits.

"Forecast”

One of the many tools available on our MAINFINEX exchange is Forecast. Selecting this button will activate a useful analytical indicator. This indicator is particularly common among the most profitable traders, and it is now available to those on our cryptocurrency exchange. This entire functionality is available at the press of a button; just select “Forecast.”

Liquidity and Profitability

We could build a mechanism into our exchange to resolve issues that long-term investors and traders face related to trading fees. Our exchange can offer flexible interest rates that vary according to the volume. This will allow those with larger trade volumes to take advantage of lower trading fees and make greater profits. We can also reduce the fees in the case of severe price deviations. The market-making traders who create liquidity will be charged fees at a lower rate. This increases liquidity by reducing slippage and volatility. In the case of price deviation, the fees will be reduced, which should trigger an increase in trading volumes. This can reduce the volatility associated with this type of price deviation.

Purchasing Liquidity

Additionally, MAINFINEX will work with other mini exchanges to help with expanding the liquidity of our exchange. Working with these partner exchanges will offer traders on our exchange increased liquidity. The partner exchange will display our exchange’s market depth on its website. All of the relevant fees will be collected in the form of our tokens, encouraging adoption and their usefulness.

Passive Income

Our passive income feature on MAINFINEX allows for the activation of a trading robot to close transactions. This robot acts according to carefully crafted algorithms that deliver proven results. Successful third-party funds will provide these trading robots. To activate this feature on our interface, simply select “Passive Income.” Our algorithmic robots will begin closing the trades for you at the appropriate times.

API for Businesses and Professional Traders

We will provide our business clients and large-scale professional traders with an API. This API offers direct access to trading. It also allows clients to send and receive trade orders while maintaining minimal delays, ideally none at all. The API will appeal to intraday trading companies, funds, trust funds, and prop trading companies, among other clients.
